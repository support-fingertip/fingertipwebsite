Real Estate Virtual Tour

Immersive 360-degree property tours that let buyers explore every room from anywhere in the world.

Immersive Experience

Properties Come Alive in 360 Degrees

Fingertip's Virtual Tour solution creates photorealistic 360-degree walkthroughs of your properties. Buyers can explore every room, check the view from the balcony, and experience the space as if they were physically there, all from their browser or VR headset.

Interactive hotspots provide property details, floor plan overlays show room dimensions, and integrated lead capture converts virtual visitors into real buyers. Perfect for pre-construction projects, international buyers, and high-volume showings.

Capabilities

Key Features

Deliver stunning virtual experiences that convert browsers into buyers.

360-Degree Tours

High-resolution panoramic imagery stitched into seamless walkthrough experiences with smooth room-to-room navigation.

VR Headset Support

Compatible with Oculus, HTC Vive, and Google Cardboard for a fully immersive virtual reality walkthrough experience.

Interactive Floor Plans

Clickable floor plans with room dimensions, area calculations, and direct links to the corresponding 360-degree view.

Info Hotspots

Embedded clickable markers that display property specs, material details, amenity information, and pricing overlays.

Lead Capture Forms

Embedded contact forms and callback requests within the tour experience, automatically synced to Salesforce CRM.

Viewer Analytics

Track which rooms visitors spend the most time in, heatmaps of attention areas, and engagement metrics per property.

Embeddable Widget

Embed tours on your website, property portals, or social media with a lightweight, responsive iframe widget.

Dollhouse View

3D dollhouse perspective showing the entire property layout from above, with smooth transitions to first-person view.

Process

How It Works

From camera capture to published tour in just days.

1

360-Degree Capture

Our photography team captures high-resolution 360-degree images of every room, corridor, and outdoor space using professional equipment.

2

Processing & Stitching

Images are processed, color-corrected, and stitched into seamless panoramas. Floor plans and 3D models are generated.

3

Interactive Enhancement

Hotspots, navigation markers, floor plan links, and lead capture forms are added to create an interactive experience.

4

Publish & Share

The tour is published to a branded URL, embedded on your website, and shared across marketing channels with analytics tracking.
