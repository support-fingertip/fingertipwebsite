Built on Salesforce Consumer Goods Cloud, our solution gives brands complete visibility across their retail distribution network. From warehouse to shelf, track every product, order, and field visit in real time.

Capabilities

Key Features

A unified platform for every consumer goods operation.

Order Management

Capture orders from field reps or distributors in real time with automated pricing, discounts, and approval workflows.

Inventory Tracking

Real-time stock levels across warehouses, distributors, and retail points with low-stock alerts and reorder triggers.

Route Optimization

AI-powered route planning for field reps to maximize store coverage while minimizing travel time and fuel costs.

Planogram Compliance

Image recognition and audit checklists to ensure in-store product placement matches brand guidelines.

Demand Forecasting

Machine learning models predict demand by SKU, region, and season to optimize production and procurement.

Distributor Portal

Self-service portal for distributors to place orders, track deliveries, view invoices, and manage returns.

Promotion Management

Plan, execute, and measure trade promotions with budget tracking, ROI analytics, and retailer collaboration.

Territory Analytics

Geospatial analytics for territory performance, market penetration, and competitive intelligence dashboards.
