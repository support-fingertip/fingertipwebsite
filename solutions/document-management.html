Document Management Solution

Digitize, organize, and automate your document workflows with enterprise-grade security and compliance.

Enterprise DMS

Go Paperless with Intelligent DMS

Fingertip's Document Management Solution transforms how organizations capture, store, and retrieve critical business documents. Powered by AI-driven OCR and integrated with Salesforce, every document is automatically classified, indexed, and linked to the right records.

From contract approvals and invoice processing to regulatory filings and compliance audits, our DMS eliminates manual handling, reduces errors, and ensures every document is accessible, traceable, and secure.

Capabilities

Key Features

A comprehensive document management platform for the modern enterprise.

Smart OCR

AI-powered optical character recognition extracts text, tables, and data from scanned documents and images with 99% accuracy.

Full-Text Search

Instant search across all document content, metadata, and tags with filters for date, type, author, and status.

Workflow Automation

Configurable approval chains, review cycles, and routing rules that move documents through your business process automatically.

Secure Storage

AES-256 encrypted cloud storage with role-based access, watermarking, and audit trails for every document interaction.

E-Signatures

Version Control

RESTful APIs and pre-built connectors for Salesforce, SAP, SharePoint, Google Workspace, and Microsoft 365.

Workflow

How It Works

From paper to digital in four straightforward steps.

1

Capture & Digitize

Scan, upload, or email documents into the system. AI-powered OCR extracts text and metadata automatically.

2

Classify & Index

Machine learning classifies each document by type and auto-populates tags, categories, and linked records in Salesforce.

3

Route & Approve

Documents are automatically routed through configured approval workflows with notifications and deadline tracking.

4

Store & Retrieve

Approved documents are archived with full-text indexing, making them instantly retrievable through search or CRM links.
