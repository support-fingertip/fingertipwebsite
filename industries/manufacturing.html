Industry Focus

Smart Manufacturing

Manufacturers face increasing pressure to reduce costs, improve quality, and accelerate time-to-market while navigating supply chain volatility. Fingertip helps manufacturing companies embrace Industry 4.0 with integrated Salesforce CRM, IoT-connected platforms, and AI-powered analytics that transform operations from the plant floor to the customer.

We connect your ERP, CRM, and operational systems into a unified digital ecosystem that drives efficiency, visibility, and informed decision-making across the entire value chain.

Pain Points

Industry Challenges

Manufacturing companies face operational and technology challenges demanding modern solutions.

Supply Chain Volatility

Global disruptions, raw material shortages, and logistics delays require real-time visibility and agile planning capabilities that legacy systems cannot provide.

Equipment Downtime

Unplanned machine failures cost manufacturers millions annually. Reactive maintenance strategies are insufficient for modern production demands.

Quality Control

Maintaining consistent product quality while scaling production requires real-time monitoring, automated inspection, and root cause analysis capabilities.

ERP-CRM Disconnect

Sales, operations, and finance teams work in siloed systems, creating blind spots in demand forecasting, order management, and customer service.

Workforce Optimization

Skilled labor shortages and an aging workforce demand digital tools for training, knowledge capture, and operational efficiency on the factory floor.

Sustainability & Compliance

Growing regulatory requirements around emissions, waste reduction, and ESG reporting demand comprehensive tracking and reporting capabilities.
